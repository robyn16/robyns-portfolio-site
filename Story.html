------------------ Design Philosophy

I believe the only way to create a rich product experience with lasting value is to deeply understand how and why people will use that end product, be it an app or a print magazine.

------------------ Process

I use a human-centered design approach involving customer research, brainstorming, sketching, and drafting at the begining of the process. Once the project’s design and purpose is thought through, I begin the final product. In using this approach, I’m able to create compelling work that is perfectly in line with the user’s needs.

------------------ About

I grew up in the wonderfully creative city of Montreal. After jumping between Montreal and Toronto, while working as an art director, I decided to enroll in a Masters program at Parsons The New School in NYC to hone my skills in brand design, strategy and managing creative teams. The past few years, I’ve worked as a freelance user experience and graphic designer for a number of clients in the big apple. After being granted a Parsons Entrepreneurial (ELab) Fellowship, I launched an online art marketplace, Art Tap Exchange, and am learning how to code to offer my clients a seamless end-to-end digital experience for their brand.
